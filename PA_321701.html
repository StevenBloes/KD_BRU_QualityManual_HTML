Hygiene of samples
(see SOP_011705 "Sampling, analysis and storage of retained samples" and PM_011721_EN "Clean sampling for human pathogenic examinations")
When sampling, gloves (preferable disposable gloves) should be worn to avoid possible contamination of the samples with human pathogens.
To avoid contamination, samples should be opened as less as possible. Counter samples should be taken directly in line with the regular sampling.
